The definition of messages that can be sent and received on the timer queue. Two types of message can be queued - messages that manipulate a software timer, and messages that request the execution of a non-timer related callback.  The two message types are defined in two separate structures, xTimerParametersType and xCallbackParametersType respectively.<br/>
