This typedef enumerates the list of available standard display monitor timings as specified in the xvidc_timings_table.c file. The naming format is:  XVIDC_VM_#lt;RESOLUTION#gt;_#lt;REFRESH RATE (HZ)#gt;_#lt;P|I#gt;(_RB)  Where RB stands for reduced blanking.<br/>
