Notes: @brief Invalidate the Data cache. The contents present in the cache are cleaned and invalidated. <br/>@return None. <br/>@note In Cortex-A53, functionality to simply invalid the cachelines is not present. Such operations are a problem for an environment that supports virtualisation. It would allow one OS to invalidate a line belonging to another OS. This could lead to the other OS crashing because of the loss of essential data. Hence, such operations are promoted to clean and invalidate which avoids such corruption.<br/>
