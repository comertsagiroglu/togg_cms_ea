PORT Pins  Summary: Identifies the available Ports pins.  Description: This enumeration identifies all the ports pins that are available on this device.  Remarks: The caller should not use the constant expressions assigned to any of the enumeration constants as these may vary between devices.  Port pins shown here are the ones available on the selected device. Not all ports pins within a port group are implemented. Refer to the device specific datasheet for more details.<br/>
