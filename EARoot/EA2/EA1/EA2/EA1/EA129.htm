SW Purpose: The purpose of sw function <b>sm_LCDErrorHandler</b> is to activate the safety action immediately for the backlight error (OR)<br/><ul>
<li>No LVDS clock (LCD_ERROR_LVDS_LOCK_FAILURE) OR</li><li>GAS (Gate All Select) Any one of the following cases triggers the GAS function (LCD_ERROR_GAS_FUNCTION_FAILURE) (OR) OR<ul>
<li>VCC lower than 2.5V OR</li><li>VSP lower than +4V OR</li><li>VSN higher than -4V</li></ul>
</li><br/></ul>
<ul>
<li>Source IC internal LD Signal fail (LCD_ERROR_INTERAL_SOURCE_CIRCUIT_FAILURE) OR</li><li>STV signal fail (LCD_ERROR_TRADITION_GATE_SIGNAL_FAILURE) OR</li><li>No video (No LVDS clock) (LCD_ERROR_SELF_PROTECTION_MODE_FAILURE) OR</li><li>Unused (LCD_ERROR_EEPROM_RELOAD_FAILURE) OR</li><li>Internal used for Hannstar (LCD_ERROR_OTP_TRIMMING_FAILURE) OR</li><li>VSP/VSN power fail (LCD_ERROR_PFM_ABNORMAL_FAILURE) OR</li><li>Glass crack detect function is not available (LCD_ERROR_GLASS_CRACK_FAILURE) OR</li><li>OTP reload fail. Driver IC will compare the reload value with one of the default register value. The fail flag will be high (LCD_ERROR_OTP_RELOAD_FAILURE)</li></ul>
